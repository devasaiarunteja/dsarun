International Conference on Innovations in Computer Science and Engineering(ICICSE-2016)

    After successful conduction of three major international conferences by Dept of CSE/IT/MCA,Guru Nanak Institutions has conducted 4th International Conference on Innovations in Computer Science and Engineering (ICICSE 2016) on 22nd & 23rd July in collaboration with Microsoft, Computer Society of India and most important Springer LNNS series publication.

The focus of ICICSE -2016 is to provide an opportunity for all the professional and aspiring researchers, scientists, academicians and Engineers to exchange their innovative ideas and new research findings in the field of Computer Science and Engineers.

The journey to the conference rolls back from Jan 2016 when our committee members started to interact with various prestigious organizations, institutions such as Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), premier local colleges, Government organizations, NIT’S, ALUMNI to convey the message of conception of the ICICSE-2016. we have been successfully able to bring several papers pertaining to vast areas of upcoming technologies. We have received research papers from several leading Universities and Countries, fellow researchers and the same speaks in volumes of our proceeding , journal and souvenir . our proceeding has got a valid ISBN no to enhance the quality of papers. The conference souvenir published all the springer paper abstracts as a memoir for the authors to carry with them their research findings.



The statistics of paper contributions to ICICSE-2016 is as follows:
No. of papers received 280
No. of papers rejected 150
No. of papers accepted 130
No. of papers published in Journal 20
No. of papers published in Proceedings 50
No. of papers published in Springer Proceedings 60

Acceptance Rate: 46.42%

We have reviewed the papers through a series of three levels of review which has its review, reviewer confidence and suggestions from our own internal review committees followed by review of Senior Academicians, Industry professionals and Professors from premier Institutions and Universities and lastly by the paramount publisher who has given a Valid ISBN no to the proceeding for validation of the paper.

As part of the conference, we have organized a CURTAIN RAISER pre-conference tutorial for the benefactor of the students, research scholars and faculties in the emerging technologies such as “SOFTWARE TEST METRICS AND PROJECT MANAGMENT “, by Manish Rai,CEO, Sho-KnackTM Advisory Services, Hyderabad, India,Ex-Nokia,Motorola consultant,USA.Digital Six Sigma Green Belt, Nominated to R&D CoE Quality Wall of Fame, FOLLOWED BY TUTORIAL on INTERNET OF THINGS by Mr. Srinivas Reddy Katta,CEO,IDEABYTES,Canada. We have received an alluring response for the pre-conference tutorials with about 1000 students, faculties ,technocrats ATTENDING from not only GNI but also from various other Institutions of Twin States.

It’s a pride factor for organizing committee to mention that 4 different sets of proceedings of the conference have been published. One with valid ISBN NO, 2 ISSUES OF JICSE ( SPECIAL ISSUE) AND MOST IMPORTANT SPRINGER LNNS SERIES . NOT TO MENTION THAT A SPECIAL VOLUME OF SOUVENIR IS BEING PUBLUSHED FOR AUTHORS OF SPRINGER TO CARRY THEIR MEMOIRS.

The inaugural function was presided by Hon’ble Chief Guest Dr. Anirban Basu, President, Computer Society of India. The guest of honors for the inaugural function were Mr. Dr. A. Govardhan, Principal, JNTUCEH Ms. G. Sandhya, Senior Program Manager Microsoft india. Mr. Raju Kanchibotla, Southern, Regional Vice President, CSI Dr. Raghav Kune, Scientist, ADRIN & ISRO

The conference was having 2 technical sessions per day, one for springer and one for special sessions for authors of journal and proceedings and all of them have been chaired and co chaired by eminent professors and doctorates .

The participants from AMITY university,pune college of engineering,GNDU Amritsar,SRM university,Chennai.Annamalai University Chennai. BMS College of engineering,Banglore.and many from Maharashtra,kerala,Andhra,telangana,tamilnadu,odisha,madyapradesh presented their research papers on 22nd july 2016.around 55 participants /faculties/research scholors/technocrats/academicians have graced the occasion on day-1 with their presentations.research papers from USA,UK,UAE,Singapore and many more countries have submitted their papers which have been published in GNIs JICSE(Journal of Innovations in Computer Science & Engineering) volume 6 issue 1.participants were provided with kits, two days lunch,accomadation at our campus ,transport facility for out station candidates.

On 23rd july 2016 i.e., Day-2 of the conference Technical sessions started at 10:30am with overwhelming response from participants from various stated of India and globe. More than 50 participants have presented their papers in the forenoon Technical Session. ICICSE-2016 was concluded by the valedictory Function with distribution of best paper awards , presentation and participation certificates and vote of thanks to all the organizing committee members,participants and management by Dr Rishi Sayal,Associate Director,GNITC.

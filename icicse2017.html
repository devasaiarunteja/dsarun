International Conference on Innovations in Computer Science and Engineering(ICICSE-2017)

     After successful conduction of four major International Conferences by Dept of CSE/IT, Guru Nanak Institutions has conducted 5th International Conference on Innovations in Computer Science and Engineering (ICICSE-2017) and 1st International Conference on Innovations in Information Technology (ICIIT-2017) on 18th & 19th August in collaboration with Microsoft, Computer Society of India, DRDO, CSIR and Springer LNNS series publication.

The focus of ICICSE-2017 and ICIIT-2017 is to provide an opportunity for all the professional and aspiring researchers, scientists, academicians and Engineers to exchange their innovative ideas and new research findings in the field of Computer Science & Engineering and Information Technology.

This year we had two surprise initiatives from department of CSE and IT. We had two conferences simultaneously executing with separate proceedings for each conference. Thanks to confidence of Springer reimposed year after year, we had proceedings of Springer of LNSS Series and Journal of Innovations in Computer Science & Engineering (JICSE) Volume 6 issue 2 & Volume 7 issue 1 for ICICSE-2017, Proceeding with ISBN indexed and new introduction of Journal of Innovations in Information Technology (JIIT) VOL 1 ISSUE 1 for ICIIT-2017.

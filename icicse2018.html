International Conference on Innovations in Computer Science and Engineering(ICICSE-2018)


     After successful conduction of five major International Conferences by Dept of CSE/IT, Guru Nanak Institutions has conducted 6th International Conference on Innovations in Computer Science and Engineering (ICICSE-2018) on 17th & 18th August in collaboration with Computer Society of India, DRDO and Springer LNNS series publication.

The focus of ICICSE-2018 is to provide an opportunity for all the professional and aspiring researchers, scientists, academicians and Engineers to exchange their innovative ideas and new research findings in the field of Computer Science & Engineering and Information Technology.

Thanks to confidence of Springer reimposed year after year, we had proceedings of Springer of LNSS Series and Journal of Innovations in Computer Science & Engineering (JICSE) Volume 7 issue 2 & Volume 8 issue 1 for ICICSE-2018, Proceeding with ISBN indexed and Journal of Innovations in Information Technology (JIIT) VOL 2 ISSUE 1.

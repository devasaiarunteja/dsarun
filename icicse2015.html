International Conference on Innovations in Computer Science and Engineering(ICICSE-2015)

     Guru Nanak Institutions where technology drives at its best, have successfully concluded the Mega event i.e. 3rd International Conference on Innovations in Computer Science and Engineering (ICICSE 2015) on 7th and 8st of August 2015. Many technocrats have graced to the city of Hyderabad to gain an enriched experience at GNI campus.

The focus of 3rd ICICSE-2015 was to provide an opportunity for all the professional and aspiring researchers, scientists, academicians and Engineers to exchange their innovative ideas and new research findings in the field of Computer Science and Engineering.

GNI has taken an innovative approach to give an enhanced platform for these personnel, participants, researchers, students and other distinguished delegates to share their research expertise, experiment breakthroughs or vision in a broad criterion of several emerging aspects of computing industry.

The journey of this International Conference begun back in January 2015 when GNI launched website for international conference www .icicse2015.org designed and developed by its students. We invited papers in the conference thru easychair . We then worked to form our committee members that started in Jan 2015 and we have interacted with various prestigious organizations, institutions such as Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), premier local colleges, Government organizations, ALUMNI to convey the message of conception of the ICICSE-2013. Our committee members started to visit various top engineering colleges like BITS-HYD, NIT Warangal, GITAM university, Bangalore University, Mysore University, Andhra University, JNTU -HYD, Universities in Chennai and so on. Further our mailing to all the colleges/university thru out India and abroad almost started in Jan end week. We have collaborated with Springer AISC series to publish papers accepted in the conference.

The organizing Team of the conference has conducted as a precursor to the event ICICSE-2015 Pre-Conference Tutorials on BIG DATA & ADVANCE ARCHITECTURE on 31 July and 1ST AUG 2015 in GNI Campus.

The Resource Person for Big Data was Dr. R.B.V.Subramanyam, Professor, Department of CSE National Institute of Technology, Warangal, Telangana & System Architecture was Dr. V. Udaya Bhaskar , founder at uDaSh Information Technologies and ex global Head- Wipro Architecture council. More than 500 Students have Participated in this preconference tutorials . Kits, study material were provided to them. Participation Certificates were handed over by the resource persons.

More than 10 Research Paper were received from various countries from USA ,Saudi Arabia, Vietnam, China and Dubai . Various Participants from other states have presented the paper in the conference.

ICICSE-2015 along with DECODE IT-Park was inaugurated by Hon'ble Minister IT & Panchayat Raj Shri K Taraka Rama Rao and Plenary session of the ICICSE 2015 was conducted by Mr. Lloyd Sanford, CEO, Top Blue Logistics.

It is our privilege that today we have been successfully able to bring several papers pertaining to vast areas of upcoming technologies and were presented during 7th and 8thof Aug.2015. We have received plenty of research papers from several leading Universities and Countries from Technocrats, Industries and researchers from academia.

Proceeding Publication

    All full papers accepted for presentation in this international conference will be Double Peer Blind Reviewed and will be published in the Springer conference proceeding with ISBN number. The accepted papers will only be published if payment of registration has been made by deadline. The conference proceeding will be sent to be reviewed for their future inclusion in the ISI Conference Proceedings Citation Index (Web of Science).


Publication Opportunity in Conference Associated Journals

Authors are also invited to submit their full paper before 10th June, 2021 for consideration for publication in conference affiliated journals . All papers of this conference will also be reviewed by respective Journal reviewing teams.

All the selected papers for the conference will be considered for publication in the special of conference associated journals International Journal of Computer Science & Engineering (IJCSE) and International Journal of Information Technology (IJIT) with an additional fee. Authors interested to publish full paper in journal can choose any one option mentioned below. Usual journal policies and peer-review policies applied.

The journal publication period is minimum 60-90 days from the date of the payment clearance against journal publication or from the date of the conference.
